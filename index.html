Biography

Lisa Nicole Lopes (May 27, 1971 â€“ April 25, 2002), better known by her stage name Left Eye, was an American rapper and singer. She was a member of the R&B woman group TLC, alongside Tionne "T-Boz" Watkins and Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas. Besides rapping and singing backing vocals on TLC recordings, Lopes was the creative force behind the group, receiving more co-writing credits than the other members. She also designed some of their outfits and the stage for their FanMail Tour and contributed to the group's image, album titles, artworks, and music videos. Through her work with TLC, Lopes won four Grammy Awards.
